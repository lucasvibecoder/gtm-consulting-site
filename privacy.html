Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Overview

Thresh ("we", "us") operates runthresh.com. This policy explains what data we collect when you visit our site and how we use it.

Information we collect

When you visit our site, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type, device, and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring URL
  • Company and professional information (via third-party identification services)

If you contact us directly (e.g. via email or a form), we collect whatever information you choose to share — typically your name, email, and company.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data about your visit:

  • RB2B — Identifies companies and professionals visiting our site using IP address and browser data. RB2B Privacy Policy
  • Vercel Analytics — Collects anonymous performance and usage data. Vercel Privacy Policy

These services may use cookies, pixels, or similar tracking technologies. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

How we use your information

  • To understand who is visiting our site and tailor our outreach
  • To improve site performance and content
  • To respond to inquiries

Your choices

You can limit data collection by:

  • Using browser privacy settings or ad blockers
  • Disabling cookies in your browser
  • Using a VPN to mask your IP address

To request deletion of any data we hold about you, email lucas.linares@runthresh.com.

Data retention

We retain visitor data for as long as it is useful for the purposes described above. You can request deletion at any time.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
