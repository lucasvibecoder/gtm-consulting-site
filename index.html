How It Works

Three steps. All verifiable.

01

I find the signal.

I identify a specific public data trigger that proves your prospect has a problem right now. Federal safety records, building compliance scores, permit filings, regulatory deadlines. Not intent data. Not job postings. Undeniable, verifiable events.

02

I build the list.

I pull prospects experiencing that signal, enrich decision-maker contacts, and score them by severity. Every record has a source you can click through and verify.

03

I write the messages.

Each prospect gets a 3-4 sentence message built from their specific data. No templates. No "I noticed your company is growing." One insight per message that makes them think "how did you know that?"
